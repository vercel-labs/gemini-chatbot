# E.U.Z Energy Construction — Employee Handbook

## 1. Company Overview

E.U.Z Energy Construction is a leading energy infrastructure company specialising in the design,
procurement, and construction (EPC) of power generation and transmission facilities. Our portfolio
spans onshore wind, solar PV, gas turbine installations, and high-voltage transmission systems.

## 2. Mission & Values

**Mission:** To deliver world-class energy infrastructure safely, efficiently, and sustainably.

**Core Values:**
- **Safety First** — No task is urgent enough to bypass safety requirements.
- **Technical Excellence** — We uphold the highest engineering standards in every discipline.
- **Integrity** — We act with transparency and accountability in all interactions.
- **Collaboration** — We achieve outcomes through teamwork across departments and disciplines.
- **Sustainability** — We minimise environmental impact in everything we build.

## 4. Code of Conduct

All E.U.Z employees are expected to:
- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism.
- Report any safety hazard, near-miss, or incident immediately.
- Protect confidential company and client information.
- Decline any gift or benefit that could be perceived as a conflict of interest.
- Raise concerns through the company whistleblower channel if necessary.

## 5. Working Hours & Leave

Standard working hours: Monday–Friday, 08:00–17:00 local time.
Site operations may apply a shift roster as defined in your employment contract.

Annual leave entitlements are detailed in your individual employment contract and the
E.U.Z Leave Policy document, available from Human Resources.

## 6. Health & Safety Obligations

Every employee is responsible for:
- Wearing required PPE in designated areas at all times.
- Reporting any unsafe condition to their supervisor or the HSE team immediately.
- Participating in toolbox talks and safety briefings as scheduled.
- Never bypassing a permit-to-work (PTW) requirement.

**Emergency Contact (Site):** Dial the site emergency number posted at all muster points.
**Medical Emergency:** Contact the on-site first aid team or dial emergency services.

## 7. IT & Data Security

- All employees must complete the cybersecurity awareness training within the first week.
- Corporate devices must be protected with a PIN/password at all times.
- Sensitive data must be stored only in approved systems — never on personal devices.
- Report any suspected phishing or cybersecurity incident to the IT Security team immediately.

## 8. HR Processes

- New employees must complete their employment paperwork within 3 business days of joining.
- Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually (mid-year and year-end).
- Training and development plans are agreed with your line manager at onboarding.
